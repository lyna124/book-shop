BOOK SHOP

the greatest ever book shop in the world

Bookshop is an online bookstore with a mission to financially support local, independent bookstores. We believe that bookstores are essential to a healthy culture. They’re where authors can connect with readers, where we discover new writers, where children get hooked on the thrill of reading that can last a lifetime. They’re also anchors for our downtowns and communities.

Ella diaries
Ella diaries These series are good to read in order … but it's not essential. The books of the Choose Your Own Ever After series are standalones with unrelated stories and characters.

The Ella diaries series explores the real issues girls face at school and home. Ella is resourceful, caring and real, and the writing is sharp and funny. With the requisite doodles, pros and cons lists, and just the right amount of snark, each book is its own juicy diary.
